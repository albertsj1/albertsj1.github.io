It's been a while

29/Dec 2015
Personal

It’s been quite a while since my last real post. Let’s just ignore that ‘Now with more Hugo’ post. :) I’ve always wanted to write about all the cool things I’m working on in my blog, but there’s just never enough time in the day.

Since my last post, I’ve left my consulting position with Covestic and hired in directly at Samsung MSCA (part of Samsung SRA) as a senior DevOps engineer working on the Milk Music project. Although I don’t care for the name ‘Milk Music’, the app is really nice. There is a native app for all recent Samsung phones, tablets, TVs and some other misc devices. You can also listen on your computer via your web browser.

I’ve been working on a lot of different technologies lately, such as: Cassandra, Hadoop, Elasticsearch, Kibana, Sensu, … Hopefully I’ll find some time to write about them soon. :)
