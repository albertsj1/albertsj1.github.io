[Grooveshark -- Listen to Free Music](http://listen.grooveshark.com/#).

Just found this website tonight for playing music online for free.  It's a community based service where you can have friends to share music with.  It's kind of like [Last.fm](http://last.fm) and[ Pandora](http://pandora.com), but with a much nicer interface.  It seems to load very quickly and the music seems like decent quality.
